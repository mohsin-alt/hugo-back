### Please contact your [Regional Manager][1] for Pricing & Scheduling.

Just a few areas we can address in trainings:

  * Pinpoint desired goals and modify the program to meet those goals
  * Generate reports based on standard mastery, time on task, and progress through lessons
  * Customize a list with lessons Designed for Arizona State AIMS test for math
  * Embed ASSET, and United Streaming Videos (if schools do not know about ASSET, etc)
  * Use educational LINKS that help teachers find more material to aid their classes
  * Emphasize and demonstrate the use of projecting the program (if schools do not have PC labs, or a classroom where each student has a PC, they can utilize the program on the WALL and use the product as their Lesson Plan)
