**Mission of UAACCE**  
The mission of the Utah Association for Adult, Community, and Continuing Education, as the primary advocate for lifelong learning in Utah, is to meet the diverse needs of all<img loading="lazy" src="http://www.uaacce.org/images/uaacce_logo.jpg" align="right" height="64" width="110" /> Utahns by providing and supporting quality educational opportunities.  
UAACCE recognizes that learning is a lifelong endeavor that takes place in many settings, people graduate from high school at both 18 and 82, individuals learn best when they are supported by their families and communities, the changing workplace requires that we constantly update our skills, and the thirst for knowledge does not dim with age.
